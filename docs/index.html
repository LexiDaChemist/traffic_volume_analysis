Discussion

Across the figures, traffic volume follows a strong time structure: weekdays tend to exhibit sharper peaks aligned with commuting, while weekends show flatter demand that often shifts later. Weather contributes additional signal, but time-of-day and day-type effects are typically larger in magnitude. This project emphasizes interpretability by using straightforward aggregation and visual evidence rather than ‚Äúblack box‚Äù results. These findings are best read as corridor-specific associations, but they offer a practical foundation for forecasting baselines and planning decisions that depend on predictable recurring demand.
